A community driven by it's people

The community is lead by Microsoft Student Ambassadors as well as students who have volunteered to make an impact through technology. Student Ambassadors are trained by Microsoft and get access to unique resources to amplify impact and bring together peers to learn new skills, solve real-world problems, and build communities across the globe.
